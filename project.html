About the proposed team project

As a group we are going to create our school's online voting system where by students dont need to queue for a long time

Here is what it is going to be like: The system is going to have a page where poeple are going to register on. Each person who wish to vote will first create an account so that their information will be store on the database so that in future when the time come for voting each person will sign in to his or her account and vote

Each person won't vote more than once and each person will have to have a secrete pin so that no one will vote for someone else without them knowing

The system will aoutomatically count votes for each of the parties

Voting will be easy and fast.Also the number of people participating in voting will be more higher than past years.

Hopefully this system will excite everyone. As a group we are happy to develop this system
